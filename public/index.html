Meet Zoë

I provide a space for reflection and rest. I am here to listen to you and to help you articulate what you are experiencing and feeling. Together we will create solutions to complex problems and leave you feeling confident that you are not alone.

I have significant and varied experience working with women in numerous contexts and take a compassionate, solution-oriented approach. I offer daytime and evening hours and am available for in home therapy in situations that require it. Feel free to reach out for a free initial phone consultation.
