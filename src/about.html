What We Do

Pregnancy is a baffling season of transition. Even the smoothest pregnancies require constant adjustment and adaptation. In many cases, pregnancy is a time with complex and difficult feelings. I provide support to women and couples negotiating these feelings as well as to those encountering prenatal anxiety and depression. I also work with those experiencing perinatal loss.

In recent years, research has been devoted to the significant challenge posed by the storm of life transitions, hormones, and emotions that we call postpartum. Every woman needs significant and multifaceted support during this time. I am passionate about providing postpartum women with attentive care. I work with women and couples experiencing postpartum anxiety, depression, difficulty transitioning into parenthood, and birth trauma.

I am available for in-home therapy for postpartum women unable to leave the home, parents in the NICU, pregnant clients on bedrest, or other situations that require it.

I am available for speaking engagements on topics related to perinatal mental health. I am a passionate advocate and educator, providing psychoeducation to couples, families, and groups.
